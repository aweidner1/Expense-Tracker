Welcome

Welcome to our expense tracker website! We will begin by asking you a few questions regarding your financial situation before we analyze your expenenditure. The expense tracker quizzes will help you break down your weekly/monthly spendings. It will then suggest certain habits depending on your spending style. We hope to make the process of money management less daunting! Add images and gallery.

Why Is Managing Money Important?

When you start managing your finances, you will have a better perspective of where and how you are spending your money. This can help keep you within your budget while increasing your savings. This will help you achieve your financial goals. Assessing priorities, checking your budget, and monitoring your progress are all things you wil learn to do through our expense quizzes. Many want financial freedom but lack the management and knowledge to do so. We are positive our quizzes will give you the knowledge to do so!
