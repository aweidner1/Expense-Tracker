Welcome

Welcome to our expense tracker website! We will begin by asking you a few questions regarding your financial situation before we analyze your expenditure. The expense tracker questionnaire will help you break down your weekly/monthly spendings. It will then suggest certain habits depending on your spending style. We hope to make the process of money management less daunting! In addition to the questionnaire, we have created an interactive budget calculator that can be used for your personal expenses!

Why Is Managing Money Important?

When you start managing your finances, you will have a better perspective of where and how you are spending your money. This can help keep you within your budget while increasing your savings. This will help you achieve your financial goals. Assessing priorities, checking your budget, and monitoring your progress are all things you wil learn to do through our expense quizzes. Many want financial freedom but lack the management and knowledge to do so. We are positive our questionnaire and budget calculator will give you the knowledge to do so!
